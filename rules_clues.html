Key Letter & Keyword

  • Every active game has a permanent KEY LETTER and a changing KEYWORD
  • The KEYWORD is a word that begins with the KEY LETTER
  • The KEYWORD is only revealed when results are released
Example:
Game: DEWDROP
Key Letter: A
Keyword: ADVANTAGE (unknown until results released)

Prediction Rules

  • Predict words using the KEY LETTER as a clue or predict randomly
  • Words must be at least three letters long (e.g., POT, CAR, BAG)
  • Longer words with morphemes have higher odds and winnings
  • Examples: DIS-ADVANTAGE-OUS, INTEN(D)-TION-AL-LLY
  • Some long words like MATHEMATICS have single morphemes
  • Minimum stake: $0.1 per word in a slip
  • Maximum words per slip: 10

Keyword Rival

A KEYWORD Rival is a word similar in spelling to the KEYWORD, provided for reference and learning.

Example:
ADVANTAGE → ADVENTURE

Result Generation

The system generates results as follows:

  • Takes the KEY LETTER of the game
  • Generates a KEYWORD from the dictionary
  • Splits the KEYWORD and adds affixes (if any)
  • Adds strange letters to the split KEYWORD
  • Produces an OVERVIEW of all possible words from the process
Example with Keyword: ADVANTAGE
Word Split: -OUS E A D V A N G E T A DIS- F -LL- Y K -SS-

Overview:
ADVANTAGE, ADVANTAGEOUS, AGENT, ATE, DAFT, DANGEROUS, DAY,
DISADVANTAGE, DISADVANTAGES, DISADVANTAGEOUS, FALL, GATE,
GET, NAG, VAN, TAG

Important Restrictions

  • Single letters cannot be doubled for advantage
  • Double letters (-LL-, -GG-, -MM-, etc.) cannot be split into singles
  • Affixes cannot be split into single letters
  • Abbreviations and acronyms (FIFA, UNESCO, USA) are void
  • Bets cannot be undone after 5 minutes
  • Bets won't be processed 5 minutes before game round ends

VirtualSpelling Rules & Clues

Game Overview

VirtualSpelling is a module focused on correctly spelling English words within time constraints.

How to Play

  • Select a target word length (L3 to L12)
  • Longer words offer higher odds and winnings
  • Correct letters are hidden under figures with strange letters
  • Select letters in correct order to spell the target word
  • Each game round lasts 120 seconds
  • 1,440 game rounds available daily
Word Length Examples:
L12: INTRODUCTION
L11: MATHEMATICS
L10: UNIVERSITY
L9: ECONOMICS
L8: ACCIDENT
L7: VEHICLE
L6: OFFICE
L5: HOUSE
L4: WIFE
L3: CUP

Game Interface

  • Target word is displayed after selection
  • Letters are hidden under figures (3-5 strange letters included)
  • Goal is to select correct letters in proper order
Example with HOUSE:
HOUSE [1][2][3][4][5][6][7][8]
Figures contain H, O, U, S, E plus 3 strange letters

SyntaxBet Rules & Clues

Game Overview

SyntaxBet involves forming meaningful dialogues from hidden letters to create complete sentences, clauses, or phrases.

How to Play

  • Form meaningful dialogues from letters 1-100
  • Dialogues must make complete sense (clause, sentence, question, statement)
  • Minimum: 3 words, Maximum: 10 words
  • Words must be arranged rightward, not leftward or random
  • No apostrophe-shortened words (use full forms)
  • Each game round lasts 120 seconds
  • 1,440 game rounds available daily
Correct vs Incorrect Examples:
❌ I'M HERE
✔ [I] [AM] [HERE]

❌ DON'T COME TO THE OFFICE TODAY
✔ DO NOT COME TO THE OFFICE TODAY

Scoring & Winnings

  • Longer words yield higher odds and winnings
  • Words are evaluated based on grammatical correctness and relevance
  • Dialogues are checked against AI dialogue bank for validity
